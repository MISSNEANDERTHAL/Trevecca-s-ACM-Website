About Us

The Trevecca ACM Chapter was born out of a need in the community. Our goal is to expose students to the world of computing machinery. Joining the Trevecca ACM Chapter allows you to not only grow in a technical but in personal and social skills as well. We encourage our members to talk to professionals in the fiels and participate in events around the community.
